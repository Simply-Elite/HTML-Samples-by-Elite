Goal 12 : Responsible Consumption and Production


What is Goal 12?

This goal is about using natural resources efficiently, reducing waste, and promoting recycling. It encourages sustainable lifestyles, green industries, and responsible business practices.

Why This Goal Matters for India

With a growing population and consumption rate, India needs to manage resources wisely. Reducing food waste, plastic pollution, and carbon footprints are crucial for environmental sustainability and economic efficiency.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Supply Chain Tracking: CS tools reduce waste in food, clothing, and manufacturing industries.
  • Waste Sorting: AI helps automate garbage segregation in smart waste plants.
  • Sustainability Dashboards: Apps help people and companies track their carbon footprint.
  • Eco Product Design: Simulations reduce packaging and promote reuse.
