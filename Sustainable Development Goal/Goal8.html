Goal 8 : Decent Work and Economic Growth


What is Goal 8?

Goal 8 promotes sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. It focuses on entrepreneurship, innovation, and protecting labor rights.

Why This Goal Matters for India

With a young workforce and growing economy, India needs to create quality jobs and support small businesses. Reducing unemployment, ensuring fair wages, and promoting safe working conditions are crucial for economic empowerment.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Job Portals: AI matches job seekers to openings through skill-based filtering.
  • Business Analytics: Startups and MSMEs use data tools to grow smarter and faster.
  • Remote Work Platforms: Tech enables flexible and inclusive employment across regions.
  • Skill Development: Online courses and AI tutors upskill youth for the digital economy.
