Goal 7 : Affordable and Clean Energy


What is Goal 7?

This goal aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. It promotes renewable energy like solar, wind, and hydropower, and encourages improvements in energy efficiency.

Why Clean Energy Matters for India

India has rising energy demands and faces pollution from coal and fossil fuels. Expanding renewable energy can reduce emissions, support rural electrification, and boost green jobs. Programs like Ujjwala Yojana and the Solar Mission are key contributors.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Smart Grids: AI optimizes electricity distribution and reduces power losses.
  • Energy Forecasting: Machine learning predicts power needs and renewable availability.
  • Home Automation: IoT manages energy use in homes, reducing waste.
  • Solar Mapping: Satellites and AI identify best locations for solar panel installations.
