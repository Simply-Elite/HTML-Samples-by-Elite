Goal 16 : Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions


What is Goal 16?

This goal promotes peaceful and inclusive societies, provides access to justice for all, and builds effective, accountable institutions at all levels. It includes reducing violence, ending abuse, ensuring legal rights, and fighting corruption.

Why This Goal Matters for India

Ensuring safety, fairness, and transparency is vital for development. India needs strong laws, better policing, and trusted governance to protect rights and promote equality. Justice systems must work for everyone, regardless of status.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Digital Courts: E-filing and virtual hearings increase access to justice.
  • Crime Prediction: AI helps detect patterns and prevent crimes in high-risk areas.
  • Blockchain: Ensures tamper-proof records in governance and law enforcement.
  • Anti-Corruption Tech: Data systems track spending and detect financial fraud.
