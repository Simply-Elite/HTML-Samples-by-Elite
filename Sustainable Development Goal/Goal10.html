Goal 10 : Reduced Inequality


What is Goal 10?

Goal 10 focuses on reducing inequality within and among countries. It aims to empower marginalized communities, ensure equal opportunities, and reduce income gaps through fair policies and inclusion.

Why This Goal Matters for India

India has deep social and economic divides. Bridging gaps in income, education, caste, gender, and rural-urban access is key to creating a just and peaceful society. Promoting diversity and inclusion benefits the nation as a whole.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Inclusive AI: Ethical AI design avoids bias and supports equal access to services.
  • Digital Inclusion: Programs bring internet and education to remote communities.
  • Language Tech: Translators and voice tools help include diverse linguistic groups.
  • Data for Equity: CS helps analyze inequality trends and shape better policy decisions.
