Goal 6 : Clean Water and Sanitation


What is Goal 6?

Goal 6 aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. It covers access to clean drinking water, safe toilets, wastewater treatment, and efficient use of water resources.

Why Clean Water and Sanitation Matter for India

India faces challenges like water pollution, shortage in rural areas, and poor sanitation in urban slums. Clean water is essential for health, dignity, and economic development. Initiatives like the Jal Jeevan Mission and Swachh Bharat Abhiyan are key steps in this direction.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Water Monitoring: Sensors and IoT detect water contamination and pipeline leaks in real time.
  • Smart Irrigation: AI helps optimize water usage in agriculture to reduce waste.
  • Sanitation Analytics: Data systems help identify areas lacking toilets and hygiene facilities.
  • Public Awareness: Mobile apps educate people on hygiene, clean water practices, and report problems.
