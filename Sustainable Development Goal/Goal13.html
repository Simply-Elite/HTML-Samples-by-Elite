Goal 13 : Climate Action


What is Goal 13?

Goal 13 urges immediate action to combat climate change and its impacts. It focuses on reducing emissions, building climate resilience, and integrating climate policies into national strategies.

Why This Goal Matters for India?

India is vulnerable to climate disasters like floods, droughts, heatwaves, and rising sea levels. Tackling climate change protects agriculture, water sources, and human health. India's global responsibility also includes pushing for renewable energy and carbon neutrality.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Climate Forecasting: AI improves accuracy of weather and disaster predictions.
  • Carbon Tracking: Tools monitor emissions from factories, vehicles, and cities.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Social media and mobile platforms spread eco-conscious behavior.
  • Green Innovation: Simulations and data modeling help build energy-efficient solutions.
