Goal 14 : Life Below Water


What is Goal 14?

This goal aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources. It includes protecting ecosystems, reducing pollution, regulating fishing, and supporting coastal communities.

Why This Goal Matters for India

With a long coastline and millions depending on marine life, India must protect its oceans. Issues like plastic waste, overfishing, and coral damage threaten biodiversity and food security. Clean oceans mean healthier people and economies.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Marine Monitoring: Satellite data and AI detect illegal fishing and pollution zones.
  • Ocean Mapping: Tech helps explore underwater life and monitor reef health.
  • Waste Detection: Drones and cameras find floating plastic and oil spills.
  • Global Collaboration: Data-sharing platforms unite scientists to protect the oceans.
