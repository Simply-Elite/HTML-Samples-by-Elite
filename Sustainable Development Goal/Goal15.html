Goal 15 : Life on Land


What is Goal 15?

Goal 15 focuses on protecting, restoring, and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. It includes managing forests, halting biodiversity loss, fighting desertification, and preserving wildlife.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Forest Monitoring: Satellites and AI track illegal logging and forest fires.
  • Wildlife Protection: Camera traps and facial recognition help protect endangered species.
  • Soil & Crop Analytics: Tech supports sustainable farming and prevents land degradation.
  • Geospatial Mapping: Helps identify protected zones and track ecological changes.
