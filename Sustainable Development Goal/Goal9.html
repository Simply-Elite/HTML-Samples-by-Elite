Goal 9 : Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure


What is Goal 9?

Goal 9 aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation. It encourages scientific research, technological advancement, and reliable transport and energy systems.

Why This Goal Matters for India

India’s future growth depends on smart infrastructure, digital connectivity, and modern industry. Better transport, innovation hubs, and clean tech create jobs and boost the economy — especially in rural and semi-urban areas.

Role of Computer Science & AI

  • Automation: Robotics and AI optimize industrial production with minimal waste.
  • Smart Infrastructure: CS powers traffic systems, bridges, and smart grids.
  • Predictive Maintenance: Sensors detect faults in roads, factories, and equipment early.
  • Digital Innovation: Platforms connect entrepreneurs and drive industrial development.
